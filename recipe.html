EGUSI'S SPECIAL RECIPE

This is a special recipe for making Egusi soup, a popular dish in Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 1 cup of palm oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cups of water or stock
  • Salt to taste
  • Seasoning cubes (optional)
  • Vegetables (e.g., spinach, bitter leaf)
  • Meat or fish (optional)
  • Chili peppers (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the palm oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they are translucent.
  3. Stir in the ground egusi and cook for about 5-10 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent burning.
  4. Add water or stock gradually to achieve your desired consistency.
  5. Season with salt and optional seasoning cubes. Stir well.
  6. If using meat or fish, add it now and let it cook through.
  7. Add the vegetables and chili peppers, cooking until they are tender.
  8. Adjust seasoning as needed and serve hot with pounded yam or rice.

Notes

Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or proteins. The key to a great Egusi soup is balancing the flavors and achieving a rich, thick consistency.

Enjoy your meal!
